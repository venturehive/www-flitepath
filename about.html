Meet BeeKeeper: Our integrated approach to managing entrepreneurship education and programs

REQUEST A DEMO

BeeKeeper is an entrepreneur education platform, created by Venture Hive. Since 2013, we have been empowering entrepreneurs with tools and intelligence. Our partners have included Microsoft, the World Bank, the Knight Foundation, the Wounded Warriors Foundation, the Department of Veterans Affairs, and the US Department of State.

Venture Hive's efforts have been saluted by the White House. We have also celebrated by PBS and Fox News for our programs to turn veterans into entrepreneurs. The Wall Street Journal, CNN, Forbes, the Washington Post and the Boston Globe have also profiled our unique approach to bringing structure and data-driven ROI to entrepreneurship education.

Headquartered in Miami, we have exported the Silicon Valley mindset to entrepreneurs on four continents, representing 50+ countries. With the launch of our BeeKeeper education platform, we are taking our business to a new level.
